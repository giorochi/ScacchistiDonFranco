About Our Organization

The Gruppo Scacchistico Parrocchiale "don Franco Venneri" organizes chess tournaments and activities for players of all levels.

The Biblioteca Parrocchiale "San Carlo Borromeo" supports our events and provides resources for chess enthusiasts.

Tournament System

Group Stage

Players are divided into groups for the initial round-robin tournament phase. Each player competes against all others in their group to accumulate points.

Knockout Stage

Top performers from the group stage advance to the knockout rounds. Matches are played in an elimination format until a champion is determined.

Board Assignments

Each match is assigned to a specific chessboard. Players can check their upcoming matches and board assignments through their personal dashboard.
